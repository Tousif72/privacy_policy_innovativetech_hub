1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Innovative Tech-Hub ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Android application Word Search Game: Brain Trainer (package name net.vizotechno.trimax.android.wordsearchinnovation_paid), a paid, ad-free word search puzzle game available on Google Play.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed to work without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, address, or phone number. The information we do process is limited to the following:

2.1 Usage & Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand how the App is used, so we can fix bugs and improve gameplay. This may include:

  • App interactions and in-app events (e.g. levels completed, screens viewed)
  • Device information (device model, operating system version, language, general region)
  • App performance data (crash reports, load times)
  • Anonymous/pseudonymous identifiers (such as an installation ID or Android Advertising ID) used solely for aggregate analytics, not to build an advertising profile

2.2 Purchase Data

The App offers in-app purchases (e.g. removing limits, unlocking content) processed through Google Play Billing. See Section 6 for details.

2.3 Support Communications

If you contact us directly (for example, using the in-app "Support" or "Feedback" option, or by emailing us), we receive your email address and the content of your message so we can respond to you.

2.4 Locally Stored Data

Game progress, settings, and preferences (such as level progress and unlocked content) are stored locally on your device. This data is not personal information and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues and crashes
  • To understand aggregate usage trends and feature engagement
  • To process and validate in-app purchases
  • To respond to support requests you send us

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use the limited data we collect to build advertising profiles.

6. Purchases

Any in-app purchases you make are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive, see, or store your payment card or Google account details — Google handles all payment information according to its own privacy and security practices. We only receive confirmation that a purchase was completed, so we can unlock the corresponding content on your device.

7. Advertising

Word Search Game: Brain Trainer is a paid, ad-free application. It does not display advertisements and does not integrate any third-party advertising SDK. No advertising identifiers are collected or used for ad targeting.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience, including younger players. We do not knowingly collect personal information (such as name, email address, or precise location) from children. The limited analytics data described in Section 2 is used only in aggregate to improve the App and is not used to identify individual children or to serve targeted advertising, as the App contains no ads.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address below so we can address it.

9. Data Retention & Security

We retain analytics and support data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. We use reasonable administrative and technical measures, including relying on Google's secure infrastructure for analytics and billing, to help protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can reset or limit your device's advertising identifier at any time in your Android device settings, even though this App does not use it for ad targeting.
  • You can request information about, or deletion of, any data associated with your support communications by contacting us (see Section 12).
  • You can uninstall the App at any time, which removes all locally stored game data from your device.
