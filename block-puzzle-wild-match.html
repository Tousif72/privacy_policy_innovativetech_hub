1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Innovative Tech-Hub ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Android application Block Puzzle Wild Match (package name com.vizotechno.blockpuzzlewildmatch), a free, ad-supported block-matching puzzle game available on Google Play.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed to work without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, address, or phone number. The information we do process is limited to the following:

2.1 Advertising Data

Because the App displays ads, our ad partner (Google AdMob) collects and processes certain data on its own behalf in order to select, serve, and measure ads. See Section 5 for details.

2.2 Support Communications

If you contact us directly (for example, by emailing us), we receive your email address and the content of your message so we can respond to you.

2.3 Locally Stored Data

Game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not personal information and is not transmitted to our servers.

The App does not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive device data, and it does not require you to sign in.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App
  • To display and measure the performance of ads that fund the free version of the App
  • To respond to support requests you send us

We do not sell your personal information. Any use of identifiers for ad personalization is performed by our advertising partner as described in Section 5, subject to your device's ad settings and, where applicable, your consent choices.

This processing is governed by Google's own privacy policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Where required by applicable law (for example, for users in the European Economic Area, the UK, and similar jurisdictions), the App presents a consent form, built on Google's consent tools, before showing personalized ads, and we honor the choices you make there.

You can manage or reset your device's advertising identifier, or opt out of personalized advertising generally, at any time in your device's ad settings (Android Settings > Google > Ads / Privacy & security > Ads).

In line with Google Play Families Policies and applicable laws such as COPPA, we do not knowingly serve personalized (interest-based) advertising to users we know to be children; only contextual, non-personalized ads are intended for that audience.
