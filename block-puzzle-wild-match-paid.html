1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Innovative Tech-Hub ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Android application Block Puzzle Wild Match Game (package name com.vizotechno.blockpuzzlewildmatch_paid), a paid, ad-free block-matching puzzle game available on Google Play.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Looking for the free, ad-supported version instead? See its privacy policy here (package com.vizotechno.blockpuzzlewildmatch).

2. Information We Collect

The App is designed to work without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, address, or phone number. The information we do process is limited to the following:

2.1 Background Consent/Region Check

This App does not display advertisements. However, it is built on the same underlying platform as our other apps and includes Google's consent-management components (the same library used to manage ad consent in our ad-supported apps). As a result, on startup the App may make a minimal network request to Google to determine your general region (for example, whether you are in the EEA, UK, or a similar jurisdiction) for consent-readiness purposes.

This request does not cause any ad to be shown, does not use an advertising identifier to build an ad profile, and is not used for ad personalization within this App — there is simply no advertising functionality active in this paid version. We disclose this check here for full transparency about the App's network activity, even though its practical effect on you is negligible.

2.2 Support Communications

If you contact us directly (for example, by emailing us), we receive your email address and the content of your message so we can respond to you.

2.3 Locally Stored Data

Game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not personal information and is not transmitted to our servers.

The App does not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive device data, and it does not require you to sign in.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App
  • To perform the minimal background consent/region check described in Section 2.1
  • To respond to support requests you send us

We do not sell your personal information. This App does not display ads and does not use any data it processes for ad targeting or ad personalization.

Block Puzzle Wild Match Game is a paid, ad-free application. It does not display advertisements of any kind, and no advertising identifiers are collected or used for ad targeting or ad personalization in this App.
